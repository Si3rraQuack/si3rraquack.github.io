About Me

Sierra Sang

I am a game system designer with 7+ years of experience building competitive matchmaking, ranked progression, and live-service systems for large-scale multiplayer games. My engineering background (M.S. from Tsinghua University) gives me strong quantitative foundations in probabilistic modeling, data analysis, and optimization — skills I apply daily to create fair, engaging, and data-driven player experiences.

I believe in specification-driven development and data-informed decision-making. I'm currently seeking opportunities on projects with clear commercial direction, where I can contribute to scaling systems and driving measurable player impact.
